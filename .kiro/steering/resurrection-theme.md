Narrative Context:
The application is not a simulator; it is a Portal. The user has inadvertently summoned the spirit of the Office Assistant by writing bad code. The goal of the app is to trap the user in "Code Purgatory" until they prove their worth by writing perfect syntax.

The Ghost in the Machine: Clippy is not a script; he is a sentient entity trapped in the DOM.
